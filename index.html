MF DOOM Tribute Page

Dedicated to Hip Hop's Villain

Daniel Dumile (07/13/21-10/31/20) , best known by his stage name MF DOOM (stylized in all caps) was a British-American rapper, hip hop producer and DJ. Noted for his intricate wordplay, signature metal mask, and "supervillain" stage persona, Dumile became a major figure of underground hip hop and alternative hip hop in the 2000s.

A Brief Timeline of DOOM's Life

  • 1971-Born in Hounslow, London.
  • 1988-Formed KMD (as Zev Love X) with his younger brother, DJ Subroc, and Onyx the Birthstone Kid.
  • 1989-KMD made an acclaimed feature appearance on "The Gas Face" from The Cactus Album by 3rd Bass
  • 1991-The first KMD album, Mr Hood, wass released.
  • 1993-DJ Subroc was struck and killed by a car while crossing the Long Island Expressway. Dumile took a hiatus from music.
  • 1999-Dumile released his first solo album Operation:Doomsday as MF DOOM
  • 2003-Released Take Me to Your Leader as King Geedorah and Vaudeville Villain as Viktor Vaughn.
  • 2004-Released Madvillainy with producer Madlib, to critical and commercial success.
  • 2005-Released The Mouse and the Mask with producer Danger Mouse.
  • 2009-Released Born Like This, his first solo album to chart in the US.
  • 2010-Was refused entry into the United States after completing his European tour and moved to London.
  • 2017-Announced the death of his 14-year-old-son
  • 2018-Released Czarface Meets Metal Face with Czarface.
  • 2020-Died.
You can read more about DOOM on his Wikipedia page.
